About

A research path between intelligence and biology.

I am interested in building agents that can perceive, model, and act in closed loops, while learning from the principles of nervous systems.

My current direction connects computational neuroscience, world models, brain-inspired intelligence, BCI, neural circuits, and autonomous behavioral systems.
